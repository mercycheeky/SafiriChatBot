    Tujenge Account

    Tujenge Account offers you an opportunity to freely build up your savings for future use or in case of emergencies. Put away a portion of your income through standing orders, contractual agreements or deposit cash whenever you want.

    Features

    • Tujenge account can be opened with any amount
    • Interest earning balance is KES 20,000 calculated annually on average monthly balances
    • Facilitates a savings plan through cash, cheque, standing order or check-off from your employer
    • 4 free withdrawals per year

    Benefits

    • Absolutely no charges
    • Loans available up to 90% of the deposit amount
    • Free statement available on request
    • Earn interest annually
    • Lower interest rate on loans secured by the deposit

    Requirements

    • Photocopy of National ID or valid passport
    • Any amount of cash to open the account
    • A free passport size photo will be taken at your preferred branch
